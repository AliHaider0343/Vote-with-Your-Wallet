Vote with Your Wallet

Enabling people to publicly express their objection to creative tax avoidance, unfair employment practices, racism, disregard of environmental impact and other unethical business and political practices. The idea is that users will be able to add their signatures under a cause, which can be created by other members, supporting with the cause and pledging not to do business or otherwise deal with the misbehaving entity highlighted in the cause.
